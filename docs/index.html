Financial leasing

ISIC Rev.5 6491

An open business blueprint for financial leasing, combining a governed Leasing-LLM advisor with an independent Leasing Governor that gates every high-stakes decision. This actor covers lease-application intake, creditworthiness and collateral disclosure proposals, and lease-funding disbursement — all backed by an immutable audit ledger and human-mandatory approval workflows.

No lease is funded without human sign-off. Hard constraints (fabricated jurisdiction requirements, incomplete evidence, insufficient collateral coverage, unresolved adverse credit flags) force holds that cannot be overridden. Deploy as a forkable OSS business: any qualified, licensed lessor can fork, customize, deploy and operate a live instance in their jurisdiction.
